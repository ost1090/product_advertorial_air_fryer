The Ultimate Australian Buyer's Guide

When investing in a glass air fryer, consider these critical factors to ensure you select the best model for your needs. Capacity is paramount, especially for larger Australian families. Look for an appliance offering over 5 litres if you entertain regularly or have a growing family. For more compact households or singles, a 3-5 litre model will suffice.

Cooking Functions can make or break your culinary experience. More functions provide versatility—think grilling, roasting, baking, and even dehydrating. A model offering multiple presets can save time and make meal prep more straightforward.

The material is another key aspect. Glass air fryers are popular for their ability to avoid chemicals found in non-stick coatings. Ensure your model is built with high-quality glass and offers dishwasher-safe components for hassle-free cleaning.

Unique Features such as dual zones or removable dividers allow you to cook multiple dishes simultaneously, optimizing time and resources. Look for air fryers that offer app connectivity or a digital touchscreen for increased convenience and user-friendly control.

Lastly, consider energy efficiency. Models operating at lower wattage can help reduce energy bills and are better for the environment.

Households focused on health should prioritise models with less oil usage, while tech-savvy individuals might appreciate additional smart features. These considerations will help you find the right balance between functionality, safety, and price.
