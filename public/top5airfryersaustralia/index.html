The Ultimate Australian Buyer's Guide

When selecting the right air fryer, there are several crucial features to consider:

  • Capacity: Determine the appropriate litre capacity based on your household size. Smaller units around 4L might suffice for singles or couples, while larger units exceeding 7L are better for families.

  • Wattage: Higher wattage means faster cooking times but may also consume more energy. Look for models around 1200W to 1650W for efficient cooking.

  • Functionality: Multi-functional models offer more value, allowing you to fry, roast, bake, and even dehydrate with one appliance.

  • Ease of Cleaning: Models with dishwasher-safe components make cleanup a breeze. Non-stick surfaces are helpful but verify they are free from harmful chemicals.

  • Footprint: Ensure the machine fits your counter space. Compact models suit small kitchens, while larger families may need to prioritise functionality over size.

Avoid models with low wattage or single functionality if versatility is essential. Households that entertain frequently or cook in large batches might benefit more from larger capacity models with dual baskets or advanced features.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the primary benefit of an air fryer over traditional frying?

Air fryers use significantly less oil, providing healthier meals while maintaining that desirable crispy texture.

How do I clean my air fryer?

Many components are dishwasher-safe; otherwise, use a damp cloth for internal wipes and ensure non-stick surfaces remain unscratched.

Can I cook frozen foods directly in an air fryer?

Absolutely! Air fryers are excellent for cooking frozen foods without the need for defrosting.

Is a higher wattage better for an air fryer?

While it often results in faster cooking, it can lead to increased energy usage; balance speed with your energy efficiency needs.

How important is an air fryer's capacity?

Capacity must match your cooking needs; larger families benefit from bigger baskets for fewer cooking cycles.

What additional features should I look for?

Consider models offering multiple cooking functions, timer settings, and adjustable temperature controls for versatile use.

Are glass air fryers more beneficial than traditional ones?

Glass air fryers provide toxin-free cooking and easier monitoring but may be more fragile.

How long does an air fryer generally last?

With regular cleaning and maintenance, a good-quality air fryer can last several years.

Do air fryers use a lot of electricity?

Most air fryers are energy-efficient compared to ovens, but higher wattage will use more energy.

Can I bake desserts in an air fryer?

Yes! Many air fryers come with baking functions for cakes and other desserts.
