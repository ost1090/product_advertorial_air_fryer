Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best capacity for an air fryer for a family of four?

For a family of four, consider air fryers with at least a 4.5 to 6-litre capacity to efficiently cook meals without needing multiple batches.

Are air fryers healthy?

Yes, air fryers use significantly less oil than traditional frying methods, making them a healthier option with up to 90% less fat.

How does Dual Zone Technology work?

Dual Zone Technology allows you to cook two different meals simultaneously in separate zones, saving time and enhancing meal diversity.

Is a glass air fryer safe to use?

Glass air fryers, like the Ninja Crispi™, often use thermal shock-resistant materials to ensure durability and safe cooking without toxins.

Can you bake in an air fryer?

Yes, many air fryers, including models like the Cuisinart XL, come with baking functions suitable for various recipes.

Are there any disadvantages to glass air fryers?

While offering a toxin-free cooking experience, glass air fryers can be heavier and more prone to breakage if not handled carefully.

What’s the difference between RapidAir Technology and traditional frying?

RapidAir Technology circulates hot air around the food to achieve a crispy finish without submerging it in oil, reducing calorie intake.

Do portable air fryers compromise on cooking features?

Portable air fryers, such as the Ninja Crispi™, offer compactness without losing essential features, making them versatile yet convenient.

Are there air fryers designed for smaller kitchens?

Yes, models like the Philips 2000 Series are designed with a compact footprint, ideal for tight spaces without sacrificing performance.

Can all air fryer parts be cleaned in a dishwasher?

Most air fryers have dishwasher-safe components, but it's crucial to refer to the manufacturer's instructions to ensure proper cleaning.
