The Ultimate Australian Buyer's Guide

Choosing the right air fryer involves understanding key features and personalising them to your household's needs. Here's what to consider:

Capacity: Evaluate your family's size and cooking habits. Smaller households might benefit from 4L+ ovens, while larger families require at least 7L.

Technology: Air crisp technology and multiple cooking presets offer versatility, allowing you to fry, roast, and bake with ease.

Material Safety: Ensure your chosen air fryer is free from harmful chemicals. Glass options and PFAS-free cookers are safer.

Ease of Use: Look for intuitive controls, and ensure components are dishwasher safe for hassle-free clean-up.

Lastly, consider your kitchen space. Smaller, compact designs are ideal for urban living, while larger models suit suburban homes with ample counter space.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size air fryer should I buy?

The size depends on your family. For individual use or couples, 3-5L is enough. Families should consider a 6L+ model.

How does an air fryer maintain a crispy texture without oil?

Air fryers utilise rapid air circulation to mimic the effect of frying, achieving a crispy texture without excess oil.

Are air fryers energy efficient?

Yes, air fryers are typically more energy-efficient than traditional ovens due to faster cooking times and concentrated heat.

Can I cook multiple food items at once?

Yes, models with dual zones or multiple baskets allow simultaneous cooking of different dishes.

Is it safe to purchase non-stick air fryers?

Choose air fryers with chemical-free coatings to avoid health risks. Glass or ceramic options are preferred.

What maintenance does an air fryer require?

Regular cleaning of the basket and pan is necessary. Many components are dishwasher safe, easing the process.

Do air fryers take longer to cook than ovens?

Generally, air fryers cook faster due to concentrated heat and smaller cooking space.

Can I bake in an air fryer?

Yes, most air fryers offer baking as a feature among their multiple cooking functions.

How do air fryers eliminate the need for oil?

While not entirely oil-free, they require significantly less oil, using air to circulate heat for cooking.

Are air fryers good for all food types?

Air fryers are versatile, handling everything from meats to vegetables, and even certain desserts.
