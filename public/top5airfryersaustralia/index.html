The Ultimate Australian Buyer’s Guide

Choosing the right air fryer involves considering several factors like capacity, functions, and ease of use. For small households or apartment living, compact models with space-saving designs are ideal. If you entertain often, larger capacity fryers with multiple functions are better suited to your needs.

Key Features to Look For:

  • Capacity: Ranges from 2 litres to over 10 litres depending on the house size.
  • Cooking Functions: More functions offer versatile meal preparation.
  • Ease of Cleaning: Dishwasher-safe parts save time and hassle.
  • Health Features: Non-toxic coatings for worry-free cooking.

For Australians focused on healthy eating, units with less oil usage and non-stick coatings are essential. Look for models that provide even crisp and flavourful results, freeing you from the guilt of deep frying.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size air fryer should I buy for a family of four?

A 4-6 litre capacity should suffice for most meal needs.

Are glass air fryers better than traditional models?

Glass air fryers offer non-toxic cooking and easy cleanup, ideal for health-conscious users.

How does air frying compare to traditional frying?

Air fryers use significantly less oil while achieving similar crispness, promoting healthier eating.

Is a dual-zone air fryer worth it?

For those cooking multiple dishes simultaneously, dual zones offer unmatched convenience.

Are the air fryer parts dishwasher safe?

Most modern air fryers have dishwasher-safe components for easy cleaning.

Can I bake in an air fryer?

Yes, many models offer baking functions alongside frying, roasting, and grilling.

Is it easy to find air fryer accessories in Australia?

Yes, most major brands provide a range of accessories for purchase online.

Do air fryers use a lot of electricity?

They are generally energy-efficient, with quick cooking times reducing power use.

Are air fryers suitable for baking desserts?

Yes, many models can bake desserts like cakes and pastries.

How do I avoid smoke from my air fryer?

Ensure the appliance is clean and not overloaded, and avoid cooking greasy foods at very high temperatures.
