Frequently Asked Questions

How does an air fryer work?

Air fryers circulate hot air around food to cook it quickly, mimicking the effect of deep frying without the oil.

Is air frying healthier than traditional frying?

Yes, air frying typically uses less oil, reducing fat and calories in meals.

How do I clean an air fryer?

Most air fryers have dishwasher-safe parts. Remove the basket and pans, clean with warm soapy water, and wipe the exterior with a damp cloth.

Can I bake in an air fryer?

Absolutely, many air fryers come with baking functions, allowing you to prepare a variety of dishes.

Are glass air fryers better?

Glass air fryers allow you to monitor food while cooking, reducing the risk of overcooking and preventing toxin leaching.

How noisy are air fryers?

Noise levels vary, but generally, they make a sound similar to a microwave. Check specific model reviews for user insights.

What's the lifespan of an air fryer?

A well-maintained air fryer can last several years. Ensure regular cleaning and follow the manufacturer’s guidelines for longevity.

Can I use aluminium foil in my air fryer?

Yes, aluminium foil can be used, but avoid blocking the airflow to ensure even cooking.

Do air fryers need to be preheated?

While many models don't require preheating, doing so can often improve cooking results.

Are replacement parts available in Australia?

Check with the manufacturer or local retailers like Amazon for replacement parts.
